"Scratch Baking at its Best!"

Welcome to Matucci's Custom Cakes!

We are a speciality cake company where we design cakes from scratch for all occasions.

David, the owner, is a trained pastry chef who has been working in the pastry field since 1978. His experience has varied from working in country clubs, restaurants and the hotel industries where he worked for Hyatt hotels as a corporate pastry chef for 12 years. He has traveled and opened pastry shops all over the United States including Puerto Rico and the Caribbean Islands. Once David relocated to Ohio, he worked as a Bakery Manager for Giant Eagle and Kroger Catering.

David has a varied amount of skills as a pastry chef which allows him to be very creative for his customers. Please allow us to create a speciality cake for you to share with your family and friends on their special occasions.

We look forward to speaking with you soon!
